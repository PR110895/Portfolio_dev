About

I build the layer people actually touch. Over the past 4+ years I've gone from frontend intern to Staff Engineer, UI Development — owning architecture, component systems, and the performance and accessibility of products end to end.

Today at Bluecopa I work on interactive analytics dashboards: complex data grids, charting, observability, and a design-system mindset that keeps everything consistent and fast. I care about clean code, smooth motion, and interfaces that get out of the user's way.

Before software, I spent four years as a civil site engineer — which is where I learned to ship things that hold up under real-world load.
